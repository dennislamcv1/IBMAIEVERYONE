Exercise: Analyzing documents with Watson Discovery!

After completing this lab, you will be able to:

  • Create a Watson Discovery service instance.
  • Utilize Watson Discovery to extract valuable information from documents.

When handling an enormous amount of text documents, Watson Discovery is a valuable tool to optimize your work flow by applying Natural Language Processing (NLP) algorithm to extract, valuable information, sentiment, concepts, semantic roles from your collection of documents.

NOTE: For learners working to get a certificate for this course (i.e. not auditing), please go through this exercise carefully and thoroughly it will be used for the graded assignment.

Pre-requisites

You will need an IBM Cloud account to do this lab. If you have not created one already, click on this link and follow the instructions to create an IBM Cloud account.

Task 1: Create a Watson Discovery Resource

  1. Login to (https://cloud.ibm.com) with your credentials. Once you successfully login to IBM Cloud, you should see the dashboard. Click on the Create resource button.

  1. You will land on the catalog page. Enter Discovery in the search field or find it in the AI category of the catalog and then click on that tile.

  2. A page will offer you to create the service. Select London as the region. Change the name of the service or keep the default, if you wish. Verify that the Lite plan is selected, and then click Create.

  1. Read the details of the various plans offered to you, and then, with the free Lite plan selected, click Create.

  2. Now, Launch your service by clicking "Launch Watson Discovery".

Task 2: Setting up Your Collection

  1. Click on "Upload your own data" to create your collection.
  1. You will be prompted to upgrade to the Advance plan, but for this exercise you only need the Lite plan, so you could set up with current plan.
  1. Since we are creating a collection for our documents, you can give your collection a name, and set the language of your documents to English.

Task 3: Uploading your Documents

  1. For the purpose of this lab, we will be analyzing IBM's 2018 Annual Report. Download the report from the following link.

Task 4: Analyzing your Document

  1. After your document has finished processing, click on the "Schema" tag highlighted by the red box.

  2. Click on document view.

  3. Scroll down the document and you can see the sentiment level of each entity. In this case it is positive for IBM Watson, IBM Services, IBM Cloud, and AI. So this tool has improved my workflow by providing me the sentiment for IBM Watson, IBM Services, and IBM Cloud without someone reading through the whole document.

Task 5: Querying your Document

  1. You can also extract important information from your document by using keywords. Click on the Search tab

  2. Enter your keywords in the Search for documents section. In this case we are searching for "Intellectual Property", Watson Discovery has returned the result for "Intellectual Property" in our document.
